### The Hormuz decoupling paradigm deepened with CNN corroboration, Iran drafting toll legislation, and the Al-Salmi VLCC attack demonstrating Iran's willingness to strike in allied waters — making permanent disruption the market's base case even with peace.

CNN independently confirmed the Wall Street Journal's March 31 bombshell: the Trump administration "believes it will have to end Iran war with oil chokepoint still closed," with White House spokesman Leavitt calling Hormuz reopening "not a core objective" (source: CNN, WSJ, White House).
