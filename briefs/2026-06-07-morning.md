The significance is the collision: only a day earlier, Mohsen Rezaei made the release of **$24bn in frozen Iranian assets *to Iran*** the non-negotiable "test of trust" for any deal. With the nuclear file now deferred to Rubio's "phase two," money *is* the core of the impasse, and the two sides are pricing the same $24bn in opposite directions.
