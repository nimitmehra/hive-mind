### Trump extended the energy strike deadline 20 hours to Tuesday 8 PM ET — the third extension since March 21 — then promised "Power Plant Day, and Bridge Day, all wrapped up in one." A 45-day ceasefire framework emerged in parallel, but mediators call its chances "slim."

The April 6 deadline that yesterday's brief flagged as THE binary event did not fire. Trump posted "Tuesday, 8:00 P.M. Separately, Axios reported a 45-day ceasefire framework under discussion — Phase 1 ceasefire, Phase 2 permanent end to war — with Pakistan, Egypt, and Turkey mediating and Witkoff texting Araghchi directly. Iran's response was categorical: Parliament Speaker Ghalibaf warned "Make no mistake: You won't gain anything through war crimes." The IRGC threatened permanent Hormuz closure "until our destroyed power plants are rebuilt" (source: Al Jazeera, JPost).

A ballistic missile struck a Haifa apartment block on April 5 carrying a cluster bomb warhead, the first confirmed use of this munition type in the war. Meanwhile, Hezbollah claimed a cruise missile hit on an Israeli warship 68 nautical miles off the Lebanese coast — the first anti-ship cruise missile claim of the war. The IDF told AFP: "We are not aware of it." The claim is unverified (source: CGTN, Arab News, Times of Israel).

### Borouge, the world's largest single-site polyolefin plant ($60 billion), shut down in the UAE — not from a direct hit, but from debris falling after successful missile interceptions.

UAE air defences intercepted every incoming Iranian missile over the Ruwais Industrial Complex on April 5 at 6:44 AM. Falling debris from those successful interceptions sparked multiple simultaneous fires, forcing immediate suspension of operations. No injuries were reported (source: Bloomberg, Khaleej Times). This is a new escalation dynamic: 100% interception rates still produce economic damage. Two other UAE facilities including Habshan gas processing had already shut down through the same mechanism in the prior 48 hours. At Khor Fakkan port, interception shrapnel injured four workers (one Nepali seriously, three Pakistanis). The UAE has now intercepted 498+ ballistic missiles, 2,141+ drones, and 23+ cruise missiles since February 28. The paradox is that perfect air defence is not the same as economic protection.
